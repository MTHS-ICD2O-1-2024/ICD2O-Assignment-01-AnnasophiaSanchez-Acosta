Claude Shannon Tribute Page

Claude Shannon

(1916-2001)

His contributions

The father of information theory. He introduced the concepts of entropy and Boolean algebra, which are foundational to digital computing and communication.
His 1948 paper, "A Mathematical Theory of Communication," revolutionized how data is encoded, transmitted, and processed.
"I visualize a time when we will be to robots what dogs are to humans."
